What is this?

Smilacin is a rare glycoside found in plants of the Smilax genus — greenbriers and sarsaparilla vines that weave through tropical forests. Rara is a vibrant Haitian carnival tradition where communities parade through streets with music, dance, and color.

In Rare Roots, you tend a garden of smilacin-producing plants. Each plot may yield a common vine or, rarely, a spectacular bloom. Collect enough rare blooms to trigger a rara carnival parade across your garden! The rarer the plant, the more smilacin it produces, and the wilder the celebration.
