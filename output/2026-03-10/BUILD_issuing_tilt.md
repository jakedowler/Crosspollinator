## Summary

**What it is:** A 45-second physics toy where colored particles issue from a central source. Tilt your device (or move your pointer) to steer gravity and guide particles into matching vessels below. Combos reward accuracy, burst waves test reflexes, and the flow intensifies as you fill vessels.

**What it isn't:** Not a puzzle. Not meditative. It's a fidget-and-flow reflex piece — direct, kinetic, satisfying.
